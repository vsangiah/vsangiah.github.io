I received my B.Tech and M.Tech in Aerospace Engineering from IIT Madras in 2020. For my Master's thesis in Computational Fluid Dynamics, I was supervised by Dr. T Jayachandran (retd. Deputy Director of VSSC, ISRO).
Following my graduation, I worked at MathWorks with the Engineering Development Group and Simulink India Quality Engineering teams till 2024.
I am currently a Master in Mathematics student at the Department of Applied Mathematics in University of Waterloo. I am investigating Summation By Parts, Simultaneous Approximation Terms and Discontinuous Galerkin frameworks for Fluid-Structure Interaction problems.
