I'm an Assistant Professor at CUNEF's the Department of Economics.

I received my Ph.D. in Economics from CEMFI in July 2019, under the supervision of Profs. Nezih Guner and Josep Pijoan-Mas.

I'm a macroeconomist working on family economics, inequality and fiscal policy. I'm also interested in the macroeconomic implications of frictions such as firing costs and price/wage stickiness.
